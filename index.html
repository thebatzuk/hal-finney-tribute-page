Hal Finney

The man who received the first bitcoin transaction

Here's a time line of Hal Finney's life:

  • 1956 - born in Coalinga, California.
  • 1979 - graduated from the California Institute of Technology, with a BS in engineering.
  • 1990s - in addition to being a regular poster on the cypherpunks listserv, Finney ran two anonymous remailers.
  • 2004 - created the first reusable proof of work system before Bitcoin.
  • 2009 - was the Bitcoin network's first transaction recipient.
  • 2014 - died as a result of complications of ALS and was cryopreserved by the Alcor Life Extension Foundation.

"The computer can be used as a tool to liberate and protect people, rather than to control them.

-- Hal Finney
